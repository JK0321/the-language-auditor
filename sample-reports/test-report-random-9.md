## Analysis

Evidence indicates the claim contained information supporting payment under the uninsured motorist coverage.

The available record further indicates that payment was not made until after judgment was entered.

The insurer's documented rationale relied upon potential litigation defenses rather than independently verified investigative findings.

Available materials indicate limited factual investigation supporting those defenses.

This report makes no determination regarding whether the insurer breached any legal duty.

---

## Findings

**Finding 1**

Independent evaluation recommended payment of policy limits.

**Finding 5**

Investigator Summary

Based upon the available materials, the investigation identified evidence supporting uninsured motorist coverage and an early recommendation to pay policy limits. The claim was not paid until after judgment was entered. The insurer's stated basis for declining payment relied primarily upon anticipated litigation defenses that, according to the court record, were supported by limited factual investigation. This report documents the investigative record only and does not reach legal conclusions regarding liability, bad faith, or statutory compliance.
